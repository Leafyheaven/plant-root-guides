Elephant Ear Indoor Care and Growth Guide

Elephant ear plants are relatively easy to maintain indoors when provided with the right balance of light, water, and humidity. Proper care ensures consistent growth and long-term plant health.

To understand both care and benefits together, you can explore this detailed elephant ear indoor benefits guide.

Basic Indoor Care Tips

  • Provide bright, indirect sunlight
  • Keep soil consistently moist but not soggy
  • Maintain warm indoor temperatures
  • Ensure proper humidity levels

Common Problems and Solutions

  • Yellow leaves → Overwatering
  • Brown edges → Low humidity
  • Slow growth → Insufficient light
